Shipping

We are same day baked goods delivery service. If your order is placed before 12noon South African time, we will deliver your order, countrywide. Alternatively, you are welcome to choose any future date for delivery.

For Johannesburg bake orders, we can deliver same day, but such orders must be placed via our call centre before 12noon. Please call to enquire.

We cannot guarantee a specific delivery time on the day, although we will make every effort to adhere to a time if such has been requested.

While we have a list of delivery countries available, should you wish to send a bouquet to a country not listed it is likely that we will still be able to do so. Please call our call centre to enquire.
